About Me

I am Naswanna Anak Asin, a Geologist from Malaysia with a Bachelor’s Degree (Hons) in Applied Science (Geoscience) from Universiti Malaysia Kelantan, awarded in 2024. I specialize in Geological Assessment, Data Analysis, Geosite Assessment, and Project Coordination, with experience in both government and private sector projects, including the Development of Karst Geosite in Bau and Geological Mapping at Gunung Gading, Lundu in Sarawak.
To accomplish my tasks effectively, I regularly use advanced geological and mapping software such as ArcGIS, Dips, MacroMedia Freehand MX, GeoRose, QGIS, and Global Mapper. Currently employed with Borneo Geoscience Sdn. Bhd. I am committed to expanding my expertise and contributing to impactful geological initiatives.
